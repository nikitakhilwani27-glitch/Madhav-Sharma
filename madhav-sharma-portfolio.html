About

01 — Profile
Madhav SharmaNew Delhi, IN

I don't just create content—I build strategies that solve real business problems.

I believe brands grow when they say things people genuinely want to hear. Rather than simply running campaigns, I build narratives that connect emotionally with audiences and create measurable impact.

Alongside content strategy, I have hands-on experience in digital marketing, enabling me to manage campaigns from ideation to execution. I've worked on multiple production shoots, both in front of and behind the camera — and I love creating campaigns that aren't just seen, but remembered.

I've worked on Click-to-WhatsApp Ad (CTWA) campaigns that helped bring in more Sales Qualified Leads, and I have solid experience in WhatsApp marketing. I've also managed brand voice through Online Reputation Management (ORM), keeping how a brand is perceived consistent and on-message across channels.
